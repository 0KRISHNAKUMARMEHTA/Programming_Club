Who We Are

Our Mission and Goals

The Quad AI Programming Club is a student-run community that helps learners of all levels build real coding skills — from beginner to advanced. We explore programming languages, problem-solving, and teamwork through fun, hands-on sessions and projects.

Our club focuses on languages and tools such as Python, JavaScript, C/C++, Git, and web technologies. Whether you’re preparing for hackathons or building your first app — we’ve got your back.

What We Value

  • Peer Learning & Mentorship
  • Open Source Contribution
  • Problem Solving & Creativity
  • Collaboration & Growth
